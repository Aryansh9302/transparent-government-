About the Transparent Government System

This sample website demonstrates how a transparent digital system for Indian governance could look. Citizens can track all types of government projects, subsidies, schemes, and contracts. Every transaction is:

  • Digitally signed by both providing and receiving parties.
  • Time-stamped and stored in a secure ledger.
  • Available in real-time, 24/7, in view-only mode to all citizens.
  • Classified as Central or State / UT projects.

Trust, Security & Digital Signatures

Digitally Signed Transactions

Every transaction is digitally signed by both the Government Department and the implementing agency / individual, ensuring authenticity and non-repudiation.

Immutable Records

Once published, records cannot be edited or deleted. Citizens can always see a tamper-proof history of project funding and usage.

Nation-First Transparency
